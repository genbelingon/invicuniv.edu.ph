Intramurals

Acquaintance Party

  • Invictus Students Organization again celebrated their annual Acquaintance Party held at Country Club Hotel last Friday, October 18, 2017. Students who shined during the party were voted by MCCID faculty and their peers. The winners received trophies, sashes and special cash prizes. Nearly 120 students, alumni, faculty and staff attended the event.
